SSH key vault

Your SSH key is encrypted at rest and only decrypted into RAM. Unlock runs vault-unlock in the terminal — type your passphrase there. It auto-locks when the tab is idle or loses focus; Lock now clears it immediately. First time? Run vault-init in the terminal.
